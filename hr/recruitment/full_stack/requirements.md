## About the Project

We are partnering with one of the largest solar energy companies in the United States to modernize and expand their core digital platforms. These platforms include both internet and extranet portals that connect extensive networks of solar panel installers and play a key role in maintaining the company’s competitive edge.

Our work focuses on building and enhancing engineering systems that cover the full lifecycle of solar installations — from design and calculation to verification, bill of materials management, and ordering. These systems support both residential and commercial projects and are key to enabling efficient, scalable renewable energy infrastructure.

As installation complexity continues to grow, so does the need for innovation. This is an opportunity to work on impactful, large-scale systems that directly contribute to the advancement of renewable energy infrastructure.
